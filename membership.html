Membership Information

Becoming a Member

We are currently accepting applications for new members. To join the Club, you make a joining contribution (refundable in most cases) and pay an annual fee for aircraft fixed costs. Regular Members can pay the annual fee in monthly increments.

Regular Plus Member

Voting rights and full aircraft access.

  • Refundable*: One-time $8,000 Membership Deposit
  • Non-Refundable: $90.83 / month membership fee

Regular Basic Member

Voting rights and full aircraft access.

  • Refundable*:
    • One-time $500 Membership Deposit
    • $25 / month loan principal up to $8,000 (can pay off early if desired)
  • Non-Refundable:
    • $90.83 / month membership fee
    • 4.5% APR of outstanding principal balance each month (starts at $28.13 / month and decreases over time)

Associate Member (Social / Family / Student)

No voting rights and cannot solo aircraft.

  • Refundable*: $20 Membership fee
  • Non-Refundable: $40 / year

* Principal Refund Policy: The bylaws outline this specifically, but basically you can get your "refundable" amount back as long as the club can afford it, or are put in a queue waiting to be paid out when a new member joins to replace you.

Membership Requirements

  • Valid FAA pilot certificate (Student pilot or higher)
  • Current medical certificate
  • Clean driving record
  • Ability to meet our insurance requirements
  • Commitment to safety and club policies

Application Process

  1. Submit an initial inquiry through our contact form
  2. Meet with the membership committee
  3. Review and sign club documents
  4. Complete membership application
  5. Pay initial fees
  6. Complete club checkout procedures
